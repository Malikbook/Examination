CUSTOM MADE CREATIVITY SINCE 1960

HANDS HEAD HEART
Nervilamp lighting products have always been renowned for their high quality as well as the exquisite nature of materials used. It’s a passion that is expressed in top quality design and attention to the smallest of details.
Nervilamp makes suspension lamps, ceiling lights, wall lights, lamps, spotlights and decorative objects.
LUXURY ARTISAN MANUFACTURING
Our vast experience has allowed the company to become the point of reference in the production of luxury interior lighting not only in Italy, but also overseas.
Our collections express creativity, sophistication, luxury and attention to quality, with a production method that complies with EC regulations for each individual project.
HIGH LEVEL OF CUSTOMISATION
Our tailored approach and ability to understand each and every need of our customers allows us to develop highly customised lighting projects.
Each creation is the result of the skilled combination of precious materials such as bronze, brass and ceramic. The high quality of Austrian crystal and ancient techniques ensure perfection in every product.

Capturing light in beautiful forms means giving it new life.
